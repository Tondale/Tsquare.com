    Ford Mustang GT (2009) and it's specs:

    Engine: 4.6-liter V8
    Horsepower: 300 hp
    Torque: 320 lb-ft
    Transmission: 5-speed manual (with a 5-speed automatic option)
    0-60 mph: ~5.1 seconds
    Top Speed: ~149 mph
    Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD)
    Weight: ~3,483 lbs
    Fuel Economy: 15 mpg city / 23 mpg highway

    Interior:

    Classic muscle car interior design
    Leather seats (available), with sportier options for GT trims
    Shaker 500 audio system (upgradeable to Shaker 1000)
    Retro dashboard with modern features
    Available navigation system and Bluetooth
    Seating Capacity: 4 passengers
    Cargo space: 13.1 cubic feet (trunk)

    Exterior:

    Iconic Mustang fastback profile
    17-inch or 18-inch wheels (GT trims)
    Hood scoop and rear spoiler (available)
    Fog lights in the front grille for the GT model
    Optional racing stripes and various color options
    Signature three-bar taillights
