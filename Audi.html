    Audi A4 (2009):

    Engine: 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 (TFSI)
    Horsepower: 211 hp
    Torque: 258 lb-ft
    Transmission: 6-speed manual or CVT (automatic)
    0-60 mph: ~6.7 seconds
    Top Speed: ~130 mph (electronically limited)
    Drivetrain: Quattro all-wheel drive (AWD) or front-wheel drive (FWD)
    Weight: ~3,483 lbs
    Fuel Economy: 23 mpg city / 30 mpg highway

    Interior:

    Luxurious, minimalist interior with premium materials
    Leather seats standard with heated options
    Multi-function steering wheel with paddle shifters
    MMI infotainment system with available navigation
    Bang & Olufsen premium sound system (optional)
    Seating Capacity: 5 passengers
    Cargo space: 12.0 cubic feet (sedan)

    Exterior:

    Sleek, elegant design with a single-frame grille
    LED daytime running lights
    Optional 18-inch or 19-inch wheels
    Sunroof (available)
    Chrome exterior accents
    Available S-line package for sportier aesthetics
