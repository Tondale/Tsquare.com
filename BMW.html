    3. BMW 3 Series (335i, 2009)

    Engine: 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-6
    Horsepower: 300 hp
    Torque: 300 lb-ft
    Transmission: 6-speed manual (with 6-speed automatic option)
    0-60 mph: ~5.3 seconds
    Top Speed: ~155 mph (electronically limited)
    Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD) or optional all-wheel drive (AWD)
    Weight: ~3,527 lbs
    Fuel Economy: 17 mpg city / 26 mpg highway

    Interior:

    High-quality materials with modern, driver-centric design
    Leather upholstery (optional), premium vinyl standard
    iDrive infotainment system (optional navigation)
    High-end audio system options (Harman Kardon)
    Multi-zone automatic climate control
    Seating Capacity: 5 passengers
    Cargo space: 12 cubic feet (sedan)

    Exterior:

    Sleek, aerodynamic design with kidney grilles
    17-inch or 18-inch alloy wheels (optional)
    Adaptive Xenon headlights
    Sunroof (available)
    LED taillights
    Chrome or black trim, depending on the package
